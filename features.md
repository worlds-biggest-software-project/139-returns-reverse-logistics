- Return reason classification and analytics
- Refund processing and automation

## Legal & IP Summary

EU WEEE Directive and Circular Economy Action Plan are regulatory frameworks driving feature requirements (data capture, compliance tracking) but not IP concerns. GS1 SSCC serialisation and carrier APIs are industry standards without patent encumbrances. IATA dangerous goods regulations and ISO 14001 are compliance standards, not IP. No software patents identified as encumbering reverse logistics or returns management platforms.
